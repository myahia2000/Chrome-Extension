## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Why does the music keep playing when I close the popup?
This is a feature! We use the Chrome Offscreen API to ensure your listening experience isn't interrupted while you browse. You can stop the audio at any time by clicking the extension icon and pressing Pause.

### The stream isn't loading, what should I do?
Ensure you have an active internet connection. If the stream still doesn't play, try pausing and playing again, or reloading your browser.

### Is this extension free?
Yes, this extension is completely free to use.
